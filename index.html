About CLE Junk Removers

Founded in 2010 by Cleveland natives Mike and Sarah Thompson, CLE Junk Removers began with a single truck and a mission to provide honest, reliable junk removal services to our community. After experiencing frustration with unreliable contractors during their own home renovation, they decided to create a service that would set new standards for professionalism and customer care in the industry.

Today, our mission remains rooted in our core values of reliability, transparency, and environmental responsibility. We're proud to be Cleveland's highest-rated junk removal service, with over 10,000 satisfied customers and a commitment to eco-friendly disposal practices. Our team of certified professionals continues to raise the bar for quality service while maintaining our local, family-owned approach to customer care.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of items do you remove?

We handle virtually all non-hazardous items including furniture, appliances, electronics, yard waste, construction debris, and general household junk. Our team is equipped to handle items of any size, from small boxes to large furniture pieces.

How do you determine pricing?

Our pricing is based on the volume of items removed and any special handling requirements. We provide free, no-obligation estimates before beginning any work, and our transparent pricing means no hidden fees or surprises.

What areas do you serve?

We serve the entire Greater Cleveland area, including all surrounding suburbs within a 30-mile radius of downtown. This includes Lakewood, Shaker Heights, Cleveland Heights, Parma, and more.

How do you handle recycling and disposal?

We prioritize eco-friendly disposal methods, recycling or donating items whenever possible. We partner with local recycling centers and charities to ensure minimal environmental impact and maximum community benefit.

Do I need to be present for the pickup?

While we recommend being present for the initial assessment and pricing, we can often complete the removal even if you can't be there. We just need clear access to the items and payment arrangements made in advance.
